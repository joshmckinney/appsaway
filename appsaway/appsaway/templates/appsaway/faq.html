Frequently Asked Questions

Is AppsAway free to use?

  • Yes! AppsAway is free of charge. There are no hidden costs, fees or services.

How do I sign up / use it?

  • Create a free account here. Then start adding applications and companies to track.

What do you do with my data?

  • Your data and privacy are of the utmost priority. Data is NEVER sold, transferred, viewed or accessed by any third parties. All data remains on a secure server. If at any time you opt to delete your account, all data is subsequently purged.

Who made AppsAway and why?

  • AppsAway was developed by Josh McKinney, a Software Development graduate in pursuit of the dream job.

I forgot my password, what can I do?

  • Please use the contact form with the subject "Forgot Password". Include your username and send it via your account's associated email address. We will get back to you as soon as possible with a regenerated password. Thank you for your understanding.
