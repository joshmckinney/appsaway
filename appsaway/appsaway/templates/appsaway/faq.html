Frequently Asked Questions

Is AppsAway free to use?

  • Yes! AppsAway is free of charge. There are no hidden costs, fees or services.

How do I sign up / use it?

  • Create a free account here. Then just start adding applications and companies to track.

What do you do with my data?

  • Your data and privacy are of the utmost priority. Data is NEVER sold, transferred, viewed, or accessed by any third parties. All data remains on the secure server. If you get a job (which we hope you do) or opt to delete your account, all data is subsequently deleted.

Who made AppsAway and why?

  • AppsAway was developed by Josh McKinney, a software development student in pursuit of the dream dev job.

I forgot my password, what can I do?

  • Unfortunately right now we haven't implemented an automated reset process.
    Please use the contact form with the subject "Forgot Password", include your username, and send it via your REGISTERED account email. We will get back to you as soon as possible with a regenerated password.
